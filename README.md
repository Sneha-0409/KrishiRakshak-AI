# 🌾 KrishiRakshak AI -  An AI-Powered Crop & Livestock Disease Diagnosis and Reporting Platform

KrishiRakshak AI is a smart agricultural healthcare platform that enables farmers to detect crop and livestock diseases using Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, and Generative AI. Farmers can upload images of affected crops or animals, describe symptoms, and receive instant disease predictions along with preventive measures and treatment recommendations.

The platform also functions as an agricultural disease surveillance system by collecting and analyzing disease reports to identify outbreak trends and generate alerts for agricultural authorities and veterinarians. By providing accessible expert guidance through mobile devices, KrishiRakshak AI aims to reduce agricultural losses, improve farm productivity, and support sustainable farming practices.
